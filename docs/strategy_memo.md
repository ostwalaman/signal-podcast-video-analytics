# Podcast & Video Content Strategy Memo

**Decision:** Ship promoted video clips through a measured rollout, beginning with Brazil and the highest-ranked content categories.

## What We Learned

The content portfolio generated 1.67 million engagement hours with a 65.5% completion rate. Video accounted for 48.4% of starts, making video format strategy material to overall content performance.

Comedy is the strongest modeled investment opportunity, with a score of 87/100 and 120.5% start growth between the first and second halves of the analysis window. Health & Fitness and Technology rank second and third. True Crime has the strongest completion rate among the leading opportunities at 68.1%, suggesting that growth and depth should be evaluated together.

## Experiment Decision

The randomized promotion experiment measured whether short-form video clips increase full-episode discovery.

| Metric | Control | Treatment | Absolute lift | Relative lift |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Full-episode discovery | 15.60% | 18.68% | +3.08 pts | +19.7% |
| Completion | 51.18% | 52.68% | +1.51 pts | +2.9% |
| Listening minutes / user | 63.12 | 65.34 | +2.22 | +3.5% |

The primary metric's 95% confidence interval is +2.12 to +4.03 percentage points. Completion and listening minutes also improved, so the rollout clears the proposed decision rule without a guardrail tradeoff.

Brazil showed the largest market-level discovery lift at +4.90 points, with a 95% confidence interval of +2.60 to +7.19 points. Great Britain's interval crosses zero, so it should not be presented as a confirmed segment win. All segment effects remain exploratory until validated through a pre-specified market-stratified test.

## Recommended Actions

1. Launch a controlled Brazil rollout of promoted video clips, with full-episode discovery as the primary metric and listening minutes as a guardrail.
2. Prioritize Comedy, Health & Fitness, and Technology for programming and promotional tests, while preserving category-specific success metrics.
3. Develop creator playbooks that pair release cadence and format recommendations with measurable audience outcomes.

## Evidence And Traceability

The dashboard's generated strategy brief attaches stable evidence IDs to every quantitative claim and exposes a downloadable claim-to-metric register. Each register entry records the displayed value and whether it originated from headline KPIs, opportunity scoring, or experiment analysis.

## Next Measurement

Run a market-stratified experiment that tests category-specific creative and placement. Measure seven-day repeat consumption and creator discovery in addition to the existing metrics. Evaluate long-term retention before expanding globally.

## Limitations

Behavioral and experiment events are synthetic and demonstrate methodology rather than Spotify performance. Opportunity scores are directional. Any investment decision should be validated using production data, stakeholder context, and operational constraints.
